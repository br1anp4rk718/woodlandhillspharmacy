Gastroenterology



A patient might be diagnosed with a wide variety of abdominal and bowel conditions, including Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis or anal fissures. No matter the problems, Woodland Hills Pharmacy can prepare a formula that relieves symptoms and treats the problem areas.
Our knowledgeable compounding pharmacists can help ensure the medication is prepared in the most appropriate dosage. Using ingredients of the highest quality that meets each individual’s unique needs.
Some of the problems we help treat: Anal Fissures Crohn's Disease Ulcerative Colitus Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) Small Intestinal Bacterial Overgrowth (SIBO)
Instances when compounding is needed: Your patient is on a medication but can’t get it because it’s either on backorder or has been discontinued; Your patient requires an different dosage or strength beyond what is commercially available; The commercially available product causes allergic reactions in your patient; Your patient can’t be compliant with the prescribed therapy.
The medical condition and/or medical history of the patient is such that it requires the use of multiple therapies combined into one medication. Our compounding pharmacist can ensure your medication is prepared in the most appropriate way, from topical creams to rectal suppositories.
