Compounding for Dentistry



Woodland Hills Pharmacy provides innovative solutions for a wide array of oral and dental prescription needs, from gag reflexes and oral conditions to drug sensitivity and anxiety issues. We recognize that no two dental patients are the same.
Unlike pharmacies that target common conditions by stocking only frequently prescribed oral medicines and therapies, our state-of-the-art facility embraces a greater range of pharmaceutical ingredients and components.
Our highly trained pharmacists meet the needs of family dentists and specialists – such as oral surgeons, periodontists and orthodontists – seeking individualized treatment solutions for their patients
