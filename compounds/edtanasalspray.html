Bio Toxin Illness

EDTA Nasal Spray

EDTA 1% Nasal Spray

Chelating PX Biofilms consist of groups of bacteria/fungi attached to surfaces and encased in a protective slime polymer matrix. Pathogens in biofilms are more resistant to antibiotics and antifungals than their free-living counterparts. Thus, biofilm-related infections are persistent and often show recurrent symptoms. Biofilms can spur the immune system to mount an inflammatory response that leads to worsening of the sinusitis symptoms.

The metal chelator EDTA in combination with polysorbate 80 (an ingredient in baby shampoo) is used to destroy biofilm which are harboring bacteria and fungi.

Available in: 2 oz. nasal spray bottle
